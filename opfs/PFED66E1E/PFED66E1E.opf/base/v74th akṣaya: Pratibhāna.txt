74th akṣaya: Pratibhāna

74th Imperishable: Eloquence.(p. 149)


de la spobs pa mi zad pa gaṅ źe na?

tatra katamat pratibhānākṣayatā?

What then is the imperishability of eloquence?

ma thos pa ’dod pa gaṅ źe na? gaṅ spobs pa so sor thob pa ste, de’i dbaṅ du byas nas de la spobs pa mi zad pa gaṅ źe na źes bya ba la sogs pa gsuṅs so – "Who is the one seeking what he has not heard (aśrutaprārthin)? The one who has attained eloquence (pratibhānapratilabdha). With reference to him (tam adhipatiṃ kṛtvā); it is said ..


spobs pa źes bya ba ni gaṅ ’di thogs pa med pa’i spobs pa daṅ,rgyun chad pa med pa’i spobs pa daṅ,mi ’phrogs pa’i spobs pa daṅ,ma rmoṅs pa’i spobs pa daṅ,myur ba’i spobs pa daṅ,dga’ ba’i spobs pa daṅ,la gor du spobs pa daṅ,mi ’gyur ba’i spobs pa daṅ,rno ba’i spobs pa daṅ,mgyogs pa’i spobs pa daṅ;

pratibhānam iti yad idam apratihatapratibhānam,anācchedyapratibhānam,asaṃhāryapratibhānam,asaṃmūḍhapratibhānam,aśupratibhānaṃ,prītipratibhānaṃ,śīghrapratibhānam,acañcalapratibhānaṃ,tīkṣṇapratibhānaṃ,kṣiprapratibhānam.

The eloquence in question is unhindered eloquence,uninterrupted eloquence,insuperable eloquence,unconfused eloquence,ready eloquence,eloquence full of joy,quick eloquence,unchangeable eloquence,sharp eloquence,immediate eloquence. [This is the essential character (lakṣaṇa) of the bodhisattva’s eloquence].

gaṅ la yaṅ brten mi dgos par bstan pas na thogs pa med pa’i spobs pa’o, bskal pa’am de las lhag par yaṅ ston pas na rgyun chad med par spobs pa’o, bdud daṅ mu stegs can la sogs pas zil gyis mi non ciṅ nams kyaṅ ñams par ’gyur ba med pas na mi ’phrogs pa’i spobs pa’o, chos daṅ don de mi brjed pas na rmoṅs pa’i spobs pa’o, dris pa rnams la lan glon pas na myur ba’i spobs pa’o, gaṅ daṅ gaṅ bstan pa rnams kyis sems kyi dbaṅ po la dga’ bskyed par byed pas na dga’ ba’i spobs pa’o, gźan dag zil gyis gnon par byed pas na la gor du spobs pa’o, ji ltar khas blaṅs pa las bsgyur te gźan du ’chad par byed pas na mi ’gyur ba’i spobs pa’o, chos kyi don myur du rtogs pas na rno ba’i spobs pa’o, zab mo’i don rtogs par byed pas na mgyogs pa’i spobs pa ste, ’di ni byaṅ chub sems dpa’i spobs pa’i mtshan ñid do.


de’i spobs pa de ni las kyi rnam par smin pas yoṅs su sbyaṅs pa,saṅs rgyas kyis byin gyis brlabs pa,lha rnams kyis yoṅs su bzuṅ ba,phyad par luṅ ston pa,’bras bu yod par chos ston pa,mya ṅan las ’das pa’i lam du ’gro ba ste;de de ltar spobs pa phun sum tshogs pa yin no.

tat tasya pratibhānaṃ karmavipākapariśodhitaṃ,buddhādhiṣṭhitaṃ,devaparigṛhītam,akṣūṇavyākaraṇam,amoghadharmadeśakaṃ,nirvāṇamārgagamanam;sa evaṃ pratibhānasaṃpanno bhavati.

That eloquence of his is purified through the maturation of earlier actions,blessed by the Buddhas,adopted by the gods,free of faulty grammar,teaching the unfailing religion,going the way to extinction;thus he becomes perfected in eloquence.

de’i źes bya ba ni byaṅ chub sems dpa’ de’i’o, de źes bya ba ni goṅ du bśad pa’i spobs pa de’o; las kyi rnam par smin pas yoṅs su sbyaṅs pa źes bya ba ni lus daṅ ṅag daṅ yid kyi las yoṅs su dag pas de’i śes rab kyi raṅ bźin spobs pa de yaṅ yoṅs su dag ces bya ba’i don to; saṅs rgyas kyis byin gyis brlabs pa źes bya ba ni saṅs rgyas kyi mthus bstaṅs pa źes bya ba’i don to; lha rnams kyis yoṅs su bzuṅ ba źes bya ba ni dris pa’i lan gdab pa daṅ bśad pa la sogs pa rdzogs śiṅ ma lus par ston pa’am zur chag ciṅ ñams pa med par ston nus pa’o; ’bras bu yod par chos ston pa źes bya ba ni don gaṅ daṅ gaṅ gi phyir chos bstan pa’i don de daṅ de dag de bźin du yod par ’gyur ba’o; mya ṅan las ’das pa’i lam du ’gro źes bya ba ni spobs pas chos bśad pa rnams mya ṅan las ’das pa thob par byed pa’i lam daṅ mthun par ’gyur ba’o; de źes bya ba ni spobs pa rnam par dag pa daṅ ldan pa’i byaṅ chub sems dpa’ de’o; de ltar spobs pa phun sum tshogs pa yin no źes bya ba ni goṅ du bśad pa lta bur na spobs pa mthar phyin pa yin no źes bya ba’i don to.


gzugs su snaṅ ba ji sñed par byaṅ chub sems dpa’ de’i mdo daṅ tshigs su bcad pa bstan pa la spobs par snaṅ ba yaṅ de sñed du,sgrar grag pa ji sñed par byaṅ chub sems dpa’ de’i mdo daṅ tshigs su bcad pa bśad pa la spobs par snaṅ ba yaṅ de sñed de;’di bstan to, ’di bśad do, źes de mi rtog ciṅ;mṅon par ’du byed pa med mod kyi, ’khor gaṅ daṅ gaṅ du ’gro yaṅ ruṅ ste, gal te rgyal rigs kyi ’khor du’am, gal te bram ze’i ’khor du’am, gal te rje’u rigs kyi ’khor du’am, gal te khyim bdag gi ’khor du’am, gal te dge sbyoṅ gi ’khor du’am, gal te rgyal chen bźi’i ’khor du’am, gal te sum cu rtsa gsum pa’i ’khor du’am, gal te ’thab bral gyi ’khor du’am, gal te dga’ ldan gyi ’khor du’am, gal te ’phrul dga’i ’khor du’am, gal te gźan ’phrul dbaṅ byed kyi ’khor du’am, gal te bdud kyi ’khor du’am, gal te tshaṅs pa’i ’khor du yaṅ ruṅ ste, de daṅ der de’i lhun gyis grub pa ñid du sems can thams cad kyi dad pa daṅ dbaṅ po rnams la chos spobs par gyur te;de ’dod na tshe gźan nam, bskal pa gźan gyi bar du yaṅ mi ldaṅ źiṅ thogs pa med par chos ston to.de’i chos bstan pa des kyaṅ de byed pa’i sdug bsṅal yaṅ dag par zad par bya ba’i phyir ṅes par ’byuṅ bar ’gyur te;

yāvanto rūpapratibhāsās tāvanto ’pi tasya bodhisattvasya sūtragāthādeśanāyāṃ pratibhānapratibhāsāḥ;yāvanti rutānuravaṇāni tāvanto ’pi tasya sūtragāthāvyākyhāne pratibhānapratibhāsāḥ.idaṃ deśayāmīdaṃ nirdeśayāmīti na kalpayati.anabhisaṃskāro ’pi yāṃ yāṃ parṣadaṃ vopasaṃkramati, yadi kṣatriyaparṣadaṃ vā, yadi brāhmaṇaparṣadaṃ vā, yadi vaiśyaparṣadaṃ vā, yadi gṛhapatiparṣadaṃ vā, yadi śramaṇaparṣadaṃ vā, yadi cāturmahārājaparṣadaṃ vā, yadi trāyāstriṃśatparṣadaṃ vā, yadi yāmaparṣadaṃ vā, yadi tuṣitaparṣadaṃ vā, yadi nirmāṇaratiparṣadaṃ vā, yadi paranirmitavaśavartiparṣadaṃ vā, yadi māraparṣadaṃ vā, yadi brahmaparṣadaṃ vā, tasyāṃ tasyāṃ tasyānābhogatayā sarvasattvaśraddhendriyeṣu dharmaḥ pratibhāti.sa ākāṃkṣamāno yāvaj janmāntaraṃ kalpāntaraṃ nottiṣṭhann apratihato dharmaṃ deśayati.iyaṃ ca tasya dharmadeśanā niryāti tatkarasya samyagduḥkhakṣayāya.

That bodhisattva’s manifestations of eloquence in teaching prose and verses are as many as there are manifestations of forms;that bodhisattva’s manifestations of eloquence in explaining prose and verses are as many as there are words sounded.He does not entertain thought-constructions like: “I demonstrate this, I teach this”.But even though he is not conditioned by anything, to whatever assembly he goes, be it an assembly of aristocrats, be it an assembly of priests, be it an assembly of craftsmen, be it an assembly of house-owners, be it an assembly of ascetics, be it an assembly of the four protectors of the world, be it an assembly of the thirty-three gods, be it an assembly of the death-king, be it an assembly of the gods in the delightful heaven, be it an assembly of the gods who take delight in changing, be it an assembly of the gods powerful in changing into someone else, be it an assembly of the Evil One, be it an assembly of Brahmā, in all those assemblies his religious teachings appear clearly in accordance with the faith and abilities of all beings, by virtue of his effortlessness [of eloquence].If he so wishes, he does not leave off even if another life elapses, or another world-age elapses, he teaches religion unhindered.And this teaching of religion of his succeeds, for the one who practises it, in truly bringing about the cessation of suffering [of beings].

gzugs su snaṅ ba ji sñed pa źes bya ba ni gzugs su snaṅ ba’i chos ji sñed pa rnams mi rtag pa la sogs par sun ’byin pa daṅ, chos de dag gi mtshan ñid tshigs su bcad de sbyar te bstan par nus pa’i śes rab kyaṅ de sñed do źes bya ba’i don to; de bźin du sgra la yaṅ sbyar ro.de daṅ der źes bya ba ni ’khor de daṅ de dag tu’o; de’i lhun gyis grub pa ñid du źes bya ba ni byaṅ chub sems dpa’ de’i spobs pa rtsol ba daṅ ’bad pa med par źes bya ba’i don to; dad pa ni ji ltar ’dod pa’o, dbaṅ po rnams ni rnon po daṅ ’briṅ daṅ tha ma rnams so, chos spobs par ’gyur te źes bya ba ni dad pa daṅ dbaṅ po daṅ mthun mthun du chos ston par byed ces bya ba’i don to.de ’dod na tshe gźan nam źes bya ba la sogs pa ni spobs pa mi zad pa daṅ ldan pa’i byaṅ chub sems dpa’ de: gal te bdag ñid ’dod na tshe du ma daṅ, bskal pa du ma ’das kyi bar du yaṅ ’dug pa’i spyod lam las mi ldaṅ źiṅ bsam mi dgos par chos ston to, źes bya ba’i don to.de’i chos bstan pa des kyaṅ źes bya ba la sogs pa la byaṅ chub sems dpa’ de’i spobs pas chos bstan pa des kyaṅ sems can rnams sdug bsṅal yaṅ dag par zad par bya ba’i phyir ṅes par ’byuṅ bar ’gyur te de bźin du sdug bsṅal yaṅ dag par zad par byed nus so źes bya ba’i don to.


’di ni byaṅ chub sems dpa’ rnams kyi spobs pa mi zad pa’o.

idam ucyate bodhisattvānām akṣayaṃ pratibhānam.

This is called the bodhisattvas’ imperishable eloquence.


btsun pa śa ra dva ti’i bu, de dag ni byaṅ chub sems dpa’ rnams kyi gzuṅs daṅ spobs pa mi zad pa’o.

amū, bhadanta śāradvatīputra, bodhisattvānām akṣaye dhāraṇīpratibhāne.

These, reverend Śāradvatīputra, are the bodhisattvas’ imperishable memory and eloquence.

gzuṅs daṅ spobs pa mi zad pa bśad zin to.
